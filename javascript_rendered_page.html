!!!FanDuel Batter Props WARNING!!!
FanDuel batter prop bets will stand and be action if the player does not start but later pinch hits (FanDuel only requires a plate apperance and other books require the batter to be in starting line-up)
  1. First, check if your player is the starting-lineup. If they are, then the FV and EV% listed is correct.
  2. If the starting lineup isn't out, yet, then you MUST run any FanDuel batter prop through the Pinch-Hit Risk Calculator (on each row in the results below) to determine it's true FV and EV%.
(Meanwhile, I will work on having CNO automatically factor this in)

!!!CNO +EV Warning!!!
The CNO +EV page works by essentially showing you the book that is a big outlier to the rest of the market. Exchanges (ProphetX, Novig, Sporttrade) often shows up as an arb to the rest of the market and so they will show up on the CNO +EV page. However, multiple users have reported being down overall on exchanges, specifically small market NBA Player Props. They show being up overall and up on every book except exchanges. It could be that exchanges have some sharp users, since exchanges are naturally sharp. It could also be really poor variance. Or a mixture of both. I have almost a years' worth of CNO historical odds/data but I still need to link it to the actual results to determine who is definitively sharp at what. Until then, it may be best to simply arb/hedge with exchanges.
See this post in the CNO Discord for more information.
